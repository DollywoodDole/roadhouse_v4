◇ Guild Architecture

Four Guilds.
One Ecosystem.

The RoadHouse operates through four specialized guilds — each with its own domain, KPI, and governance mandate within the DAO framework.

📡
Media Guild
Content Creation
The front-facing engine of the RoadHouse. Responsible for all streaming, short-form video, VOD clipping, social distribution, and international translation of content. Every view begins here.
KPI: Monthly Reach ● Recruiting
  • Live streaming on Kick — long-form, unfiltered
  • Short-form content on TikTok (@roadhousesyndicate)
  • VOD clipping and highlight distribution
  • Multilingual content translation
  • Social media management across platforms
⚙️
Builder Guild
Technology & Dashboards
The technical backbone. Builders develop and maintain the community platform, tokenomics tooling, streaming analytics, and the on-chain infrastructure that powers the entire DAO governance layer.
KPI: Uptime & DAU ● Forming
  • Community dashboard development (this site)
  • $LUX token smart contract deployment
  • AI-assisted content moderation systems
  • Streaming analytics platform
  • On-chain analytics (Year 2)
🏕️
Frontier Guild
Events & Compound
Anchored in Saskatchewan's physical landscape. The Frontier Guild manages real-world infrastructure — compound operations, event production, merchandise logistics, and the physical community space that grounds the RoadHouse.
KPI: Event Revenue ● Planning
  • Saskatchewan compound site acquisition (Year 2)
  • Physical events and festival appearances
  • Merchandise production and fulfillment
  • Community compound infrastructure
  • In-person member meetups
📈
Venture Guild
Treasury & Investments
The capital arm. The Venture Guild deploys DAO treasury via governance-approved proposals, sources deals, performs due diligence, and manages the portfolio. First investment targeted at $25k CAD in Year 2.
KPI: Portfolio IRR ● Seeding
  • Treasury deployment via DAO governance votes
  • Deal sourcing and due diligence
  • Portfolio company management
  • Grant co-application coordination
  • Investor relations and LP reporting
Governance via Snapshot & Aragon
All proposals follow a structured lifecycle: Idea Phase (7 days) → Temperature Check → Formal Proposal → 5-day Vote (≥10% quorum) → On-chain Execution via Aragon / Gnosis Safe 3-of-5 multisig → 30-day Post-execution Review. Any Regular+ member can propose.

⬡ $LUX Token

Internal Economics.
Governance & Utility.

$LUX is a governance and utility token. No public sale will proceed without formal securities law opinion from qualified Canadian counsel. Pre-launch distribution via SAFT to accredited investors under NI 45-106.

Token Allocation
Community 25%
DAO Treasury 25%
Creators 22%
Founders (4-yr vest) 18%
Partners & Advisors 10%
Token Utility
🗳
Governance
Snapshot-weighted voting on treasury deployments, guild proposals, and strategic partner deals. 5-day windows, ≥10% quorum required.
🔒
Access & Tier Gating
Membership ladder gating (see §Membership). Higher tiers unlock compound access, deal flow, and IP licensing rights.
💰
Revenue Share
Pro-rata distribution of designated treasury surplus to Steward-tier and above holders. Governed by DAO vote on each distribution.
⏳
Staking
Lockup incentives rewarding long-term alignment. Target velocity V < 5 annually — low velocity = higher token value retention.
⚠ Compliance Note
$LUX is structured as a utility/governance token. No public sale without formal securities opinion (Canadian + US counsel). Budget: $15k–$25k Year 1. Pre-launch distribution via SAFT — accredited investors only (NI 45-106).

◈ The Code

Six Principles.
No Compromises.

The RoadHouse selects for people who understand that the most powerful room is the one that never appears on social media.

🤐
Discretion
What's discussed in the RoadHouse stays in the RoadHouse. Loose lips have no business here. It's not a policy — it's a personality trait.
⚡
High Standards
We don't lower the bar. Everyone at the table earned their seat through quality and commitment. No shortcuts, no cargo-culting.
🌐
Global Reach
From Saskatchewan to Milwaukee to the internet. The RoadHouse spans disciplines and borders. First-principles thinking travels.
🔬
First Principles
Synthesizers. Physics. Code. Built from the ground up. We ask why before we ask how, and we never assume someone else has it figured out.
🤝
Community
Not a fanbase — a network. Talent is recognized, supported, and put to work on things that matter. Guild membership is earned through contribution.
🥃
Good Taste
In bourbon, coconut vodka, music, and people. One Smooth Ride. The standard isn't arbitrary — it keeps the room worth being in.
